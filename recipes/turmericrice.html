Turmeric Garlic Rice

Description

This turmeric rice recipe is light, fragrant, and is a perfect companion to any meat dishes. It is super easy to make and provides you with delicious and flavourful rice.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Basmati rice
  • 1/4 Tsp ground turmeric
  • 2 cloves of minced garlic
  • salt and pepper (to taste)
  • 1 cup water

Steps

  1. In a small saucepan, combine your rice and some water (do not measure this)
  2. Swirl the rice in the water as it gets cloudy
  3. Strain the water out and put more water in (repeat steps 1-3 until the water is clear)
  4. Now in your pan with your washed rice, add your measured water
  5. Put in your minced garlic and turmeric
  6. Cover the saucepan and turn the heat to medium-high
  7. Let the water get to a boil and turn the heat down to medium-low
  8. Cook until the water dries out or until the rice is cooked to your desired texture
  9. Strain your rice if needed, plate, and enjoy!
