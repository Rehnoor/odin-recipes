Rehnoor's High Protein French Toast

Description

This high protein french toast recipe is a very customizable, delicious, and healthy recipe for gym buffs like myself. It is very simple and can be put together very fast for a filling breakfast. This recipe yields 2 pieces of french toast.

Pretty much anything in the recipe can be customized. You can use any type of bread to yield 2 pieces of French Toast. For the nut butter, you can use any nut butter you prefer (even Nutella!). You may adjust the amount of eggs to get more protein or make your toast more "eggy". As for milk, you may use any percent of cow milk or any alternatives you prefer. As for the fruits, I like to use bananas or strawberries but feel free to use any fruit!

Ingredients

  • 4 Pieces of bread
  • 2 Tbsp Peanut/Almond Butter
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 Tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 1/4 Tsp Ground Cinnamon
  • 1/4 Cup of Milk
  • 1 Cup of sliced Strawberries
  • Maple Syrup (optional)

Steps

  1. Get a wide bowl and crack two eggs in there.
  2. Add in the bowl your milk, cinnamon, and vanilla extract and whisk together
  3. Get your four pieces of bread and spread your nut butter on each slice
  4. Sandwich the four pieces together to make a Peanut/Almond butter sandwich
  5. Heat a nonstick pan and spray with cooking oil. While you wait for the pan to heat, coat the sandwiches in the egg blend.
  6. Once the pan is hot, put both pieces into the pan.
  7. After waiting 30 seconds, press down and flip the french toast
  8. Pour ontop any of the remaining egg mixture
  9. Now, cook both sides of each french toast until toasted to your desired look
  10. Once cooked, place both toasts in your plate, top with strawberries and enjoy!
