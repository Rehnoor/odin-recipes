Rehnoor's High Protein Scrambled Eggs

Description

This simple and boring recipe for scrambled eggs has taken inspiration from the famous Gordon Ramsay's scrambled eggs recipe. I love the original recipe, but I like to add some extra protein to truly make it mine.

Ingredients

  • 3-4 eggs (adjust based on protein/fat goals)
  • A knob of buttah (as Gordon says)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup cottage cheese
  • 1/4 cup of chopped veggies

Steps

  1. In a cold pan, crack in your eggs and do not turn on the heat
  2. Put in your knob of butter and start whisking the eggs with a soft spatula (wooden/silicone)
  3. Turn on your heat to medium low and continue to whisk constantly. Keep agitating the eggs like I agitate my girlfriend. The key is to not let your eggs stay still in the pan for too long.
  4. If your eggs start cooking more, movie them on and off heat while continuing to stir
  5. Once your eggs are starting to clump and get the texture of mayo, take them off the heat
  6. Stir in your salt, pepper, cottage cheese, and veggies
  7. Now add back to the heat and continue stiring
  8. Continue to stir and cook until the eggs have reached your desired consistancy. Once done, plate and enjoy!
